# Part 4: AI Solution Design for a Business Problem

## Project Overview
This repository contains the AI solution design for a critical business problem in the **Healthcare** domain. As an AI Business Analyst, the objective is to formulate a strategy that leverages computer vision to improve patient outcomes and operational efficiency.

The proposed solution focuses on **Medical Image Triage**, utilizing Deep Learning (Convolutional Neural Networks) to automatically prioritize urgent medical scans, reducing the manual backlog and decreasing average resolution times.
